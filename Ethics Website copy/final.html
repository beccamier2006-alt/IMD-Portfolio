Why is it important to know which websites to trust?

Many scam websites are created with the intent to steal data from its visitors.

Some information that can be stolen online includes:

  • Financial info
  • Social security number
  • Medical records
  • Passwords
  • IP address
  • etc

How can I practice online safety?

Fortunately, we live in an era of online-awareness. More information is being shared on how to spot a scam and what to avoid. Unfortunately, older generations are known to be more susceptable to these types of scams and are targeted due to a lack of knowledge.

Some ways to practice online safety includes:

  • Never accepting cookies/terms and conditions without reading through them first
  • Don't click on any suspicious links without being 100% sure where they lead
  • Avoid putting any personal information on ANY site unless you know for a fact what will be done with it
  • If you notice a scam report it to get the site taken down
